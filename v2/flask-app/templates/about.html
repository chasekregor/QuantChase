Quantified Chase

Creator:

Chase Kregor

Goal:

The goal of Quantified Chase was to create a dashboard of my life.

Background:

Quantified Chase was insipired by the Quantified Self movement. Upon buying an apple watch, I started to fall in love with the Health App, now that I had an Apple Watch I was feeding it all this interesting data about myself 24/7. I wondered if there was a way I could share all this data other people? This made me think about all the other various platforms that contained data about myself. And so I created Quantified Chase for three reasons:

  • Display my data to others for accountability
  • Aggregate all my data for information gain
  • A fun data science project

Data:

The data comes directly from my HealthKit.

Disclaimer: Please don't do anything innapropriate with my data.

How Quantified Chase Works:

Healthkit -> QSAccess App -> Flask App -> Heroku
